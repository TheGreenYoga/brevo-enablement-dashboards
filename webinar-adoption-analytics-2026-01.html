🔬 Methodology & Caveats

Webinar date: 26/01/2026 (analysis window opens 27/01/2026 per instruction, closes 23/06/2026).

Group assignment: GSheet Company IDs (Planhat internal _id). 490 registrant rows with a Company ID parsed; 136 unique AB companies, 327 unique C companies. ~48% GSheet coverage (many registrants use personal emails not matched to Planhat accounts). Group D = all Planhat customers (status=customer, excl. ⚫Sub-account) not in AB or C (n=931).

H2 keyword: Opportunity flagged as relevant if: (1) custom.Product array contains "Insights", or (2) title contains "analytics" or "insights" (case-insensitive). Octomedia's "Renewal+ Analytics" matches by title only (Product = Price increase) — flagged accordingly. Sub-accounts excluded by company name.

Pagination: 985 total opportunities retrieved across 5 Planhat queries (OFFSET 0, 200, 400, 600, 800). OFFSET 800 returned 185 records confirming dataset completeness through June 23, 2026.

Causal caveat: Attendees are self-selected — companies that watched the webinar likely already had higher Analytics interest. The 2.8× AB vs D delta is a correlation, not causation. The "Webinar" tag on Beauty Coiffure's opp suggests webinar content generates indirect commercial signals even outside the registered audience.
