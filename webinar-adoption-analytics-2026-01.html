Key Findings

📈

4.4× lift for watchers

Companies that watched the webinar (live or replay) created Analytics/Insights opportunities at 4.41% vs 1.01% control baseline over a 5-month window.

🎯

Watching matters, registering doesn't

Group C (70 companies, registered no-shows) produced 0 opportunities. Intent without engagement produces no measurable signal above baseline.

Methodology

Registrant source (authoritative): Livestorm XLSX exports specific to the Jan 26, 2026 event — FR: 142 registrants, EN: 134 registrants, 263 unique after deduplication (team members excluded). These are the sole source for group AB / C classification.

Company ID resolution: GSheet 1SiAgj_GY70gtZeP6YbxAwH_3R5fK6-ypbYR31HEUyzk used to map registrant emails to Planhat Company IDs. 234/263 registrants resolved (89%). 29 without Company ID contribute to attendance counts but not to group classification.

Group definitions:

  • AB — at least one contact from the company attended live or watched the replay (per XLSX)
  • C — registered company, zero attendance or replay (per XLSX)
  • D — all Planhat customers (status=customer, excl. sub-accounts) not in AB or C (≈1,188)

H2 query: All Planhat opportunities createdAt > 2026-01-27T00:00:00.000Z, paginated (985 total, 5 pages). Retained if “Insights” or “Analytics” in custom.Product or title. Sub-accounts and test entries excluded.

Causal caveat: Self-selection bias applies. Webinar registrants are already more Analytics-interested than average customers. The AB vs D delta is a correlation, not proof of causation.
