Key Findings
🚫 Zero Churn Among EverAfter Users
Among the 106 Pro clients who completed at least one EverAfter onboarding stage, not a single one churned during Jan–May 2026. Groups B and C show 3.6% and 2.6% churn respectively.
🤝 Less Manual CSM Intervention
Group A required 14.2% manual CSM touches (call+note) vs 20.3% for Group B and 17.9% for Group C — a 5–6 pt difference. Clients who engage with EverAfter are more self-sufficient.
📊 Stronger Phase Progression vs Never-Opened
70.8% of Group A clients have reached Success & Growth, vs 64.1% for those who never opened EverAfter. Group A also has the lowest % stuck in Onboarding (10.4% vs 17.9% for C).
⚠️ The Activation Gap
Only 29.4% of new Pro clients actually engage with EverAfter content. 38.2% open it but complete no stage. Converting openers into stage-completers is the highest-impact intervention available.
