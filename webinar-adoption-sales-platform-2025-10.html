Key Findings

📈

3.5× lift for watchers

Companies that watched the webinar (live or replay) created Sales Platform opportunities at 9.09% vs 2.57% control baseline over an 8-month window. Small AB sample (33 cos) — interpret with caution.

🎯

No signal for no-shows

Group C (38 companies, registered no-shows) produced 0 Sales Platform opportunities. Consistent with Analytics Studio: intent without engagement produces no measurable signal above baseline.

🔥

High D demand — broad market pull

34 of 37 retained companies are Group D (non-registrants). Sales Platform appears to have strong product-led demand independent of the webinar. The multiplier reflects an existing market, not just webinar-driven uptake.

⚠

H1 blind spot

No Planhat Workflow trigger exists for Sales Platform seat activation. Full funnel measurement blocked until a dedicated bot is created for first CRM/Sales seat activation event.

Methodology

Registrant source (authoritative): Livestorm XLSX exports specific to the Oct 21, 2025 event — FR: 46 registrants, EN: 49 registrants, 93 unique after deduplication. These are the sole source for group AB / C classification.

Company ID resolution: GSheet 1S9WHPifR9x7k8k2n1eiklLzyGIBNwwLfLLSbXKGUy20 used to map registrant emails to Planhat Company IDs. 73/93 registrants resolved (78.5%). 20 without Company ID contribute to attendance counts but not to group classification. Coverage gate (≥20%) met.

Group definitions:

  • AB — at least one contact from the company attended live or watched the replay (per XLSX)
  • C — registered company, zero attendance or replay (per XLSX)
  • D — all Planhat customers (excl. sub-accounts) not in AB or C (≈1,323; estimated as total Pro base ≈1,394 minus 71 XLSX-resolved companies)

H2 query: All Planhat opportunities createdAt > 2025-10-21T00:00:00.000Z, paginated (~1,540 total, 8 pages). Retained if “Sales platform”, “Sales CRM”, or “Sales” in custom.Product or title. Sub-accounts and test entries kept (JZapata flagged).

H1: Slack search in #csm-client-updates for “sales platform” returned only generic Planhat notifications and human CSM messages — no feature activation bot trigger exists. H1 = N/A.

Causal caveat: Self-selection bias applies. Webinar registrants are already more Sales Platform-interested than average customers. The high D opp count (34) confirms broad product-market pull independent of the webinar. The AB vs D delta is a correlation, not proof of causation.
